How Can You Help Raise Awareness?


  • There are lots of ways you can help raise awareness around this cause. Below are some ways:

  • Start a local group to raise awareness in your community.
  • Educate pet store owners about the inhumane conditions and cruelty of puppy mills.
  • This site explains how the UN included the internet in the Universal Declaration of Human Rights and links to lots more resources.
  • Support pet stores when they agree to stop selling puppies from puppy mills.
  • Encourage pet stores to deal only with rescue and shelter dogs.
  • adopt don’t shop shirtWear an adopt don’t shop shirt to spread the message.
